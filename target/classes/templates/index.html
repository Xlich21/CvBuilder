Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Alexandru Fenichiu

A software developer at the beginning of the road, I’m changing my career and having the experience of manager in the field of logistics and insurance. My background in logistics and insurance inform my critic but competitive approach.

Since I had my first computer, in 1999, I was fueled by my passion for understanding the nuances of programming language. I’m considering myself a self learner, eager to both build on my academic foundations in computer languages and stay in tune with the latest frameworks and updates through continued coursebook and professional development.

In 2018 I decided to begin an advanced Java course at Link Academy, where I learned Java, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL and frameworks like SPRING and JavaFX. My hunger for knowledge, determination and passion for coding made be begin and successfully finished 2 projects: To Do List (Spring) and CV presentation app (SPRING + JS). I also work on an Inventory Management App (JavaFX + Scene Builder).

I believe that hard work, determination, doing what you love and passion are keys to success – a tenet I live out through my interests in technology, gaming and creating stuff.
